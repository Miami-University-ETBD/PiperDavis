What is Gals on the Go?

Gals on the Go is a travel platform designed specifically for women who want to explore the world with confidence, adventure, and community. Founded by Piper Davis, a passionate traveler with over a decade of experience in travel consulting, Gals on the Go offers resources, tips, and a supportive network to help women travel safely and affordably.

We believe that every woman deserves the chance to explore the world on her own terms, whether that's embarking on a solo adventure, traveling with friends, or joining a group trip with like-minded women. We focus on providing a platform that offers practical travel tips, real-life stories, and a community of support for women at every stage of their travel journey.

Our Core Values

At Gals on the Go, our values are the foundation of everything we do. These values guide our mission to empower women to travel safely and confidently:

  • Safety First: Your safety is our priority. We provide up-to-date safety tips and advice for all types of travel.
  • Inclusivity: We believe that every woman, regardless of background or budget, deserves the opportunity to travel.
  • Adventure: We encourage women to step out of their comfort zones, try new things, and embrace the unknown.
  • Respect: Respect for cultures, environments, and other travelers is key to fostering meaningful experiences.

Our Community

Gals on the Go is more than just a travel platform—it’s a thriving community of women supporting each other, sharing stories, and connecting through the love of travel. Here's how we help foster that sense of connection:

  • Forums: Ask questions, share tips, and discuss your travel experiences with other women travelers.
  • Events: Attend meet-ups and gatherings around the world, designed to help you connect with fellow travelers.
  • Social Media: Stay connected and inspired through our social media channels, where we share travel stories and tips.
  • Group Travel: Join like-minded women on group trips for a safe, fun, and enriching travel experience.

Being part of our community means you're never alone in your travel journey. We believe in the power of women helping women, and we’re here to make your travel dreams a reality!
