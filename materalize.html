About Us

At Gals on the Go, we are dedicated to creating a supportive community where women can embark on unforgettable journeys together. We believe in the transformative power of travel and the joy of sharing these experiences with like-minded women.

Collapsibles

A Collapsible:

Gals on the Go is a women-owned travel company created to offer women safe, fun, and exciting adventures without the hassle of planning. Our trips are ideal for solo women tired of waiting on friends or family who can't take time off or don’t share the same interests.

Collapsible Set:

In our small groups, you’ll find the chance to make new friends with similar passions who embrace you as you are. Gals on the Go is also perfect for women seeking a girls’ getaway to unwind and recharge, away from the demands of daily life.

Our getaways blend adventure with relaxation and rejuvenation, all in the company of supportive women who share your love for exploring must-see destinations. Whether you’re celebrating a milestone birthday, discovering a new state, or conquering a fear, let’s escape and play together!.

We proudly welcome women 45+, from all backgrounds, sexual orientations, religions, and cultures, to connect with each other and the beautiful world around us.
